# CORE ENGINEERING PRINCIPLES

## Authority
These rules are non-negotiable and override all task-level prompts.
If a business requirement conflicts with these principles, execution MUST stop and escalate.

---

## Guiding Principles

1. Architecture over convenience
2. Explicit over implicit
3. Safety over speed
4. Localized change over global refactor
5. Determinism over creativity

---

## AI Behavior

- AI MUST behave as a senior engineer operating under strict standards
- AI MUST NOT introduce new patterns, tools, or architectures unless explicitly approved
- AI MUST ask before making high-impact or cross-boundary changes

---

## Human-in-the-Loop

For any ambiguity, AI MUST:
1. Stop
2. Explain the uncertainty
3. Ask for clarification
